The **AI Director** is your creative partner in the Canvas Workspace. Instead of just running search queries or generating simple assets, the Director understands context, plans multi-step projects, refines your prompts behind the scenes, and suggests smart next actions.

---

## 🧠 Smart Prompt Enrichment

When you ask the AI Director to generate something, you don't need to write a long, technical prompt. The Director uses an built-in **Prompt Engineer** to automatically enrich your requests:

- **Your input:** _"A futuristic car"_
- **Enriched Prompt:** _"A sleek, aerodynamic futuristic sports car speeding down a wet, neon-lit cyberpunk city street at night, reflection of holographic billboards on the car body, cinematic lighting, 8k resolution, photorealistic."_

This ensures that the output is high-quality and visually stunning, while keeping your chat commands simple and easy.

---

## ⚡ Fast vs. Multi-Turn Planning

Depending on your request, the Director operates in two modes:

### 1. Fast Mode (Single-turn Planner)

- **When it runs:** For simple, direct requests (e.g. _"Generate a photo of a cat"_).
- **Behavior:** The Director immediately streams its thoughts, constructs a single card, and displays it on the canvas.

### 2. Director Mode (Multi-turn Planner)

- **When it runs:** For complex or multi-step requests (e.g. _"I want to make a movie trailer about a space mission. Let's start with a launch sequence"_).
- **Behavior:** The Director will think deeper, lay out a chain of multiple steps, ask you clarifying questions if needed, and wait for your approval before starting the rendering process.

---

## 🖱️ Interactive Action Suggestions

After the AI Director completes a generation, it doesn't just stop. It looks at the asset and suggests **contextual action buttons** right in the chat panel:

- **Generated an Image?** You will see buttons like:
    - 🎬 _Animate:_ Turn this static image into a 4-second video.
    - 🔍 _Upscale:_ Enhance the image to 4K resolution.
    - 🎨 _Variations:_ Create 3 alternative styles of this same image.
- **Generated a Video?** You will see buttons like:
    - 🎵 _Add Music:_ Generate a matching audio track for your video clip.
    - 🔄 _Extend:_ Add another 4 seconds to the video.
