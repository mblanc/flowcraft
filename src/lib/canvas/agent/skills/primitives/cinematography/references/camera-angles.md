## Camera Angle / Position

Angle communicates power, vulnerability, and perspective independent of subject size.

| Shot Type                | Camera Position                     | Visual Effect                                   |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Eye-level                | Lens at subject's eye height        | Neutral, natural, relatable                     |
| Low angle                | Lens below subject, tilted up       | Power, dominance, menace, heroism               |
| High angle               | Lens above subject, tilted down     | Vulnerability, smallness, surveillance          |
| Dutch angle              | Horizon tilted 10–30°               | Unease, disorientation, psychological tension   |
| Aerial / bird's-eye view | Directly overhead or drone altitude | Scale, geography, pattern, god-like perspective |
| Worm's-eye view          | Extreme low, near ground level      | Dominance of subject, distortion, reverence     |
| Ground level             | Camera flat on the ground           | Raw intimacy with floor-level subjects          |
| Hip / waist level        | Lens at hip height                  | Hands, holsters, action near the waist          |
| Knee level               | Lens at knee height                 | Walking, dancing, movement dynamics             |
